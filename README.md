# 12-V-DC-power-input-protection-circuit.
## 12 V Input Protection Diode

For the 12 V DC input protection circuit, the 1N4007 diode was selected.

The 1N4007 diode has a maximum reverse voltage rating of 1000 V, which is much higher than the 12 V supply used in this circuit. This provides a large safety margin and ensures the diode will not break down during normal operation.

The diode also supports a maximum forward current of about 1 A, which is more than sufficient for small electronic circuits that typically draw much less current.

Another reason for choosing the 1N4007 is its low cost and wide availability. It is one of the most commonly used rectifier diodes and is easy to obtain from most electronic component suppliers.

Because of its high voltage rating, adequate current capacity, and low cost, the 1N4007 is a suitable and practical choice for protecting a simple 12 V DC input circuit.
